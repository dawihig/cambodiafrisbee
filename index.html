About the Event

Where Sport Meets
Community

Join us for an exciting morning of Ultimate Frisbee, skills clinics, fun competitions, and community activities — all completely free and open to everyone. Our mission: to share the joy of frisbee with the students and families of Phnom Penh, and inspire a new generation of players across every school in the city.

🎯
Skills Clinics

Learn throwing, catching, and strategy with hands-on coaching from experienced players.

🏆
Mini Tournaments

Teams of all ages compete in friendly, spirited matches throughout the morning.

🎉
Prizes & Giveaways

Sponsor prizes, frisbees, school kit, and surprises for participants of all skill levels.

🤝
Community Spirit

Connecting government schools, independent schools, and international families — all on one field.
